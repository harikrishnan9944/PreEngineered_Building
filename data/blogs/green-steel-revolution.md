# The Green Revolution: Sustainable Steel & Net-Zero Construction

The construction sector accounts for a significant portion of global greenhouse gas emissions. As an engineering leader, Shree Nivi Buildtech is fully committed to reducing the carbon footprint of industrial structural works.

## Sustainable Steel Sourcing

Traditional blast furnace steel relies heavily on coal. The future lies in **EAF (Electric Arc Furnace)** manufacturing.
- **Recycled Feedstock**: We prioritize sourcing steel manufactured using recycled scrap metal. This reduces the energy required for steel production by over 60%.
- **Low-Carbon Transport**: By optimizing logistic shipping and fabrications locally, we minimize carbon emissions related to material haulage.

## Eco-Friendly Building Features

Modern industrial buildings can be active contributors to environmental protection rather than passive carbon polluters.

1. **Integrated Rooftop Solar Systems**: Our PEB structures are engineered to handle the load of solar panels. We design roof pitches to maximize solar capture, turning warehouses into clean energy generators.
2. **Daylight Harvesting Skylights**: We install high-strength polycarbonate skylights covering 5% to 10% of the roof area. This reduces the need for artificial lighting during daylight shifts.
3. **Natural Ventilation Systems**: We install ridge ventilators and wind-driven roof fans to exhaust heat without using electrical cooling systems.

### Comparison of Structural Footprints

- **Traditional Concrete Structure**: High carbon cement production, long curing schedules, low recyclability.
- **Modern Steel PEB Structure**: 95% recyclable material, rapid assembly, minimal site water usage, and lower foundation mass.

> "Green engineering is not just about saving energy; it is about building structures that can be fully recycled and repurposed at the end of their lifecycle." - Viswanathan K. N.

### Shree Nivi Buildtech Goals

By 2028, we aim to transition to 100% low-VOC coatings, expand solar readiness on all warehouse layouts, and reduce steel scrap waste during cutting processes to below 1.5% using custom computer nesting layouts.
